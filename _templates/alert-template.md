## Translation Notes

When translating:
- Short sentences (under 15 words)
- Common words
- Keep lists
- Keep warnings
- Grade 6 max

## Customization Guidelines

### Required

Include:
- Date, time
- Agency
- Areas
- Deadline
- Shelter
- Transport
- Phone

### Optional

If relevant:
- Pets
- Medical
- Closures
- Routes

### Avoid

Skip:
- Legal text
- Tech words
- Many phones
- Web checks
